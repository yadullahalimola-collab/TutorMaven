Our Brand Story

TutorMaven was not born from a simple idea — that tutoring should be as organized and transparent as modern technology allows. We saw how students struggled to find reliable tutors, and how teachers found it hard to manage their classes, attendance, and payments. So, we built a bridge between both worlds — a smart, reliable platform that simplifies education management.

From small coaching centers to individual mentors, TutorMaven helps every educator stay focused on teaching while automating the rest.

Our Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize private education in India by giving tutors and students the digital tools they deserve. TutorMaven is more than a platform — it’s a movement towards structured, trustworthy, and efficient learning. We aim to make education a space where technology enhances human connection rather than replacing it.

How TutorMaven Helps

  • ✅ Simplifies class management for tutors with attendance and fee tracking.
  • ✅ Gives students verified, trusted tutors matched to their subjects.
  • ✅ Provides a clean monthly subscription system — no hourly confusion.
  • ✅ Offers verified tutor badges for transparency and trust.
  • ✅ Creates real-time connection between tutors and learners.

TutorMaven makes education more accountable — empowering both sides to focus on what really matters: learning and growth.
